ePilepsy Connect

Content Strategy

This site is a valuable resource for individuals with epilepsy and intellectual challenges. Discover resources and join group meetups/events to connect with a supportive community.

Find Resources

Here users can find all the health centers and hospitals for their treatment in their local community. This will include maps, website links, trainings and so on.

Find Your Community

Users will be able to find links to other websites or groups that are catered to specific demographics like adults with epilepsy.

Get Involved

Users will be able to find volunteering opportunities provided by other organizations as well as be part of meetup groups when the members of the site will start organizing one in the near future.

About Us

This page will include information about me and the rest of the developers as well as our stories.
