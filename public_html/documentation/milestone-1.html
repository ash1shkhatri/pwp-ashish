Epilepsy Connects

Purpose

To connect people with epilepsy, providing a platform where they can make friends and share stories, hobbies, and experiences in Nepal. This inclusive community welcomes individuals with epilepsy and intellectual disabilities, while also encouraging connections with abled adults.

Audience

The primary audience includes people with epilepsy and intellectual disabilities in Nepal. Additionally, abled adults are invited to join, fostering a diverse and supportive community where friendships can thrive.

Goal

The goal is to create a friendly hub for individuals with intellectual disabilities to share their stories, find companionship, and make friends. Through this platform, we aim to provide a space where meaningful connections can be formed.

Persona: Ravi


Hi, I am Ravi, 27 years old, living in Lalitpur, Nepal. I have intellectual disabilities and have been dealing with seizures since I was 1.


Despite my limitations, I enjoy making friends and hanging out in the afternoon. My interests include watching movies, solving puzzles, and origami. I live two blocks east of Patan Hospital.


If you are around here or want to chat, feel free to reach out. Let's connect.
