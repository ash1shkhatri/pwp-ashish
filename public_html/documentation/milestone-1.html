Epilepsy Connects

Purpose

The purpose of this platform is to serve as an inclusive and informative space for individuals with epilepsy and intellectual disabilities. Through curated content and shared experiences, this site aims to raise awareness, combat social neglect, and foster a sense of understanding and community.

Audience

The site is designed for Nepali individuals:

  • Facing epilepsy and intellectual disabilities.
  • Seeking information, connection, and support.
  • Interested in local and online meetup opportunities.
  • Open to fostering understanding and empathy within a supportive community.

Goal

The primary goal is to provide valuable information and resources to raise awareness about epilepsy and intellectual disabilities. Furthermore, it aims to address social neglect through shared experiences, facilitate connections by providing details about local and online meetup groups, and curate resources such as articles, videos, and support links. Additionally, this site strives to foster a sense of understanding and empathy within the broader community. Ultimately, this site aims is to encourage communication and connection beyond the platform, promoting lasting friendships and support networks.

Persona


Name: Ravi KC

Age: 27


Description:


Ravi is a 27 years old individual from Nepal. He has epilepsy along with intellectual disabilities. He has been dealing with seizures since the age of 1.
Despite his limitations, Ravi enjoys making friends and hanging out in the afternoon. His other interests include watching movies, solving puzzles, and origami.


Technology:


Ravi uses a Windows 7 desktop computer. He uses Samsung Galaxy 5 as his personal phone. Despite his challenges, he can surf around the internet, play videos on Youtube, and play mobile games on his phone.


Profession:


Unemployed


Attitudes and Behaviors:


Ravi lives with his parents, dealing with epilepsy and needing frequent rest due to medications. In his waking moments, he enjoys playing phone games, watching YouTube videos, and talking to his siblings. Seeing the world with the simplicity of a nine-year-old, Ravi openly expresses his feelings, oblivious to social nuances. The greetings and brief chats from neighbors and relatives bring him immense joy, adding warmth to his daily life.


Frustrations and Needs:


Ravi encounters considerable challenges in making friends and forming connections due to his intellectual disability. His difficulty in conversing like an adult often results in a lack of attention, making it challenging for him to enjoy the simple pleasure of hanging out with friends. This frustration arises from a fundamental need to socialize and share stories, a desire shared by every human being. Unfortunately, societal stigma towards disabled individuals in Nepal intensifies these challenges, making it arduous for Ravi to find the companionship he seeks. Despite these hurdles, Ravi remains resilient, searching for a platform that not only recognizes his challenges but also offers an opportunity to connect with others who comprehend and value his unique journey.


Goals:


Ravi's goal is to connect with individuals, whether intellectually disabled or not, using this platform. His vision involves utilizing the site to discover meetup groups, fostering in-person connections, and making friends beyond the digital realm. With a desire to extend his social circle and build lasting connections, Ravi looks to this platform as a stepping stone for creating genuine friendships and shared experiences outside of the online space.


User Story:


As a user, I want to connect with other individuals and make friends to hang out and spend time with.
